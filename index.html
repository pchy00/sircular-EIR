What is the Environmental Impact Rating?

The EIR is a quick way to understand the environmental footprint of a product’s packaging—from recyclability to landfill contribution.

Scores run 1–5 stars based on material type, ease of separation, compostability and more.

Compare like-for-like packaging—e.g. bottled drinks vs. cereal boxes.

Rating Guidelines

5/5

★★★★★

Highly sustainable: recyclable, compostable mono-materials

3/5

★★★☆☆

Moderate impact: mixed materials or limited recyclability

2/5

★★☆☆☆

Low sustainability: non-recyclable or complex packaging

Services

Service Agreement

Form agreements with councils & businesses, collect & process soft plastics, and deliver certified reports.

Waste Diversion Guarantee

100% of collected plastic avoids landfill—converted into biochar, bio-oil or syngas for reuse.

Sustainability Reporting

Provide ESG metrics, CO₂e emissions data, and recovery certificates for investor & public reporting.

Education Program

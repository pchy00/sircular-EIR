Empowering Sustainable Choices

The Environmental Impact Rating (EIR) is a simple 5-star system that helps consumers identify how eco-friendly a product’s packaging is — making sustainability accessible and actionable at a glance.

The Problem

Single-use plastic packaging plays a key role in food preservation but has a massive environmental cost. In Australia, 95% of plastic packaging is used once and discarded. This presents a challenge — balancing convenience with sustainability in the food industry.

Our Solution: The EIR Label

What does this mean?

EIR evaluates packaging based on:

  • ♻️ Recyclability
  • 🔄 Use of mono-materials
  • 🧴 Recycled content
  • 🌱 Compostability

Why It Works

Like the Health Star Rating system, EIR makes sustainability transparent. Public visibility leads to industry reform — just like Nutri-Grain’s and Milo’s reformulations after public scrutiny.

Educating Everyone

We make packaging literacy easy — through shelf signage, digital guides, and QR scans. EIR targets entry-level and homebrand lines first, ensuring scalable and equitable awareness.

Take Action

Let’s make sustainability visible. Let’s make informed choices easy. And let’s empower consumers to lead the change — because one label can shift the system.

What is the Environmental Impact Rating?

The Environmental Impact Rating (EIR) is a quick and easy way to understand the environmental footprint of a product's packaging — from recyclability to how it contributes to landfill.

The EIR score ranges from 1 to 5 stars, helping shoppers make better, greener choices. This score is calculated based on factors such as material type, ease of separation, and whether it's compostable or recyclable.

You can compare similar product packaging with the EIR system. For example, comparing bottled drinks or cereal boxes — but not a plastic tub to a soft pouch.

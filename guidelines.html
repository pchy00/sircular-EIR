EIR Packaging Guidelines

High EIR (4–5★)

Key Features:

  • ✔ Fully recyclable in yellow‐bin
  • ✔ Mono‐materials (e.g. PP or PET)
  • ✔ >30% post‐consumer recycled content
  • ✔ Certified compostable

Impact:

  • • Low carbon footprint
  • • Diverts waste from landfill
  • • Supports closed‐loop systems

Examples:

rPET Water Bottle
Compostable Mailer
Mono‐material Yoghurt Tub
BioPak Sugarcane Tray
Moderate EIR (2–3★)

Key Features:

  • ✔ Partially/conditionally recyclable
  • ✔ Small material mixes
  • ✔ 10–29% recycled content
  • ✔ Unverified compost claims

Impact:

  • • Sorting challenges
  • • Potential contamination

Examples:

Mixed‐Material Tray
Alu‐Plastic Pouch
“Biodegradable” Wrapper
Low EIR (0–1★)

Key Features:

  • ✖ Non‐recyclable plastics (e.g. PVC)
  • ✖ Multi‐layer that can’t be separated
  • ✖ 0% recycled content
  • ✖ Not compostable

Impact:

  • • High landfill/ocean pollution
  • • Lost recovery opportunities

Examples:

Foil‐Plastic Chip Bag
Polystyrene Foam
Mixed Snack Wrapper

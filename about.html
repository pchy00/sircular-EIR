About the EIR Project

Empowering everyday sustainability decisions through smart packaging labels.

Why We Created EIR

Our team is passionate about reducing plastic waste and encouraging more informed, sustainable purchasing decisions. The Environmental Impact Rating (EIR) is our way to make packaging sustainability visible and easy to understand.

Inspired by Health Star & Energy Star labels, we realized a clear, star-based rating was missing for everyday packaging — so we built EIR to fill that gap.

Our Mission

To build a future where sustainability is second nature. EIR is not just a rating — it's a movement to make waste reduction and eco-conscious living easier for everyone.

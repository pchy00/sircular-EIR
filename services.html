Our Services

Waste-as-a-Service | Sustainability Reporting | Circular Economy Solutions

How It Works

Service Agreement

We collect soft plastic waste, process it with advanced technology, and provide certified waste-diversion & carbon-offset reports.

Waste Diversion Guarantee

100% of collected plastic avoids landfill and is converted into biochar, bio-oil, or syngas for reuse and energy.

Sustainability Reporting

Receive recovery certificates, CO₂e emissions data, and ESG metrics to share with stakeholders.

The Value of What We Create

Biochar

Sold to farmers & councils for soil improvement, water retention, and carbon credits.

~$500–$1,500/tonne

Bio-oil

Used by industrial plants or blended into renewable fuels.

~$300–$600/tonne

Syngas

On-site energy source to power operations, reducing costs and emissions.

Cost-saving fuel source
